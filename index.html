Mr. Beast's Life

MrBeast, whose real name is Jimmy Donaldson, is one of the most famous YouTubers in the world, known for turning wild ideas into massive, feel-good entertainment. He started making videos as a young teenager in North Carolina, experimenting with gaming and challenge content before blowing up with over-the-top stunts like counting to 100,000. What really sets MrBeast apart is how he mixes creativity, persistence, and kindness, building an empire around making people smile.

Philanthropy

Beyond entertainment, Jimmy is known for massive charitable initiatives. He co-founded Team Trees, raising over $20 million to plant 20 million trees, and Team Seas, aiming to remove 30 million pounds of trash from the ocean. His channel "Beast Philanthropy" dedicates 100% of its revenue to feeding the hungry and helping those in need.
